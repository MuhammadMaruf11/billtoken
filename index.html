About $BILL

BILL is a token built on the Ethereum Blockchain and is used to gain access to NoRug.com. NoRug is an exclusive launchpad that gives BILL holders access to safe and unruggable meme coins.

The only way to access the NoRug Platform is to hold BILL in your wallet. The more BILL you hold in your wallet, the more access you will have based on a Tiered System which is still in development.

Every time you join a Pre-Sale you will have to burn some BILLS, this makes BILL super deflationary. The more NoRug is used, the more BILLS will be burnt.

Burning BILLS to access NoRug is very beneficial to all BILL holders, as less BILLS become available, it increases scarcity and gradually increases the price of each BILL benefitting all holders.

Tokenomics

Billion Token (BILL) launched on August 5th 2021. There are only 1,000,000,000 BILLs in circulation and no more can be created. We felt that offering a low amount of capped tokens would enable a ‘store of value’ offering, which compliments BILLs use as a currency to access NoRug.com. Billion Token was first deployed on the Ethereum main-net as an ERC token.

We have since launched on BSC to offer more accessibility to new buyers. All founder wallets have been locked on team.finance along with liquidity which had been locked for 12 months. The initial liquidity offered was a USDC/BILL pairing and the amount of liquidity given was $10,000 which positioned the initial price at $0.00001
